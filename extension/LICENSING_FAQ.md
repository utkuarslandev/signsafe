# Licensing FAQ

## Is this project open source?

This repository is source-available under BSL 1.1. It is not an OSI open-source
license at this time.

## Can I use SignSafe personally?

Yes. Personal use is allowed under the Additional Use Grant in `LICENSE`.

## Can I use SignSafe for research or education?

Yes. Academic and research use is allowed under the Additional Use Grant.

## Can my company evaluate SignSafe internally?

Yes. Internal evaluation and internal security operations are allowed, as long as
you are not offering SignSafe as a commercial service to third parties.

## Can I run a paid service built on SignSafe?

Not under the default repository license. You need a commercial license.

## Can I fork and modify SignSafe?

Yes, but your usage must still comply with the BSL 1.1 terms and Additional Use
Grant in `LICENSE`.

## Does this license grant use of the SignSafe name/logo?

No. Trademark rights are not granted by the repository license.

## What happens on the Change Date?

Per `LICENSE`, rights transition to the listed Change License for each version on
its Change Date.

## Is this legal advice?

No. This FAQ is informational only. Consult qualified legal counsel for legal
interpretation and compliance decisions.
